### Target Market  
Axtria primarily serves Life Sciences companies, including emerging pharma, commercial and clinical teams, and organizations focused on Real-World Evidence (RWE) and Health Economics and Outcomes Research (HEOR). It also targets Global Capability Centers (GCCs). Its solutions support data management and analytics for commercial, clinical, social, patient, and medical affairs applications. Notable client impacts include a $25 million business impact for a top biopharma company through enhanced data science capabilities, an 8% revenue increase for a top 5 pharma company using MarketingIQ, and a 10% higher ROI for a top 10 pharma company via omnichannel engagement.
